BEEF-BULALO
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 kg (about 2.2 lbs) beef shanks with bone marrow
  • 2 liters water
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis) or salt, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
  • 2-3 corn on the cob, halved or cut into thirds
  • 1 bunch bok choy or pechay (Chinese cabbage), leaves separated
  • 200g green beans, trimmed
  • Optional: 2 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and halved
  • Optional: Knorr beef cube or beef broth for additional flavor
BEEF BULALO TOTURIAL
PROCEDURE

  1. In a large pot, combine the beef shanks, water, chopped onion, minced garlic, fish sauce (or salt), and whole black peppercorns. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 2 to 3 hours or until the beef is tender. Skim off any foam or impurities that rise to the surface during simmering.

  2. Once the beef is tender, add the corn on the cob, potatoes (if using), and continue to simmer until the vegetables are almost tender, about 10-15 minutes.

  3. Add the green beans and bok choy (or pechay) to the pot and cook for an additional 5 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked but still crisp.

  4. Taste the broth and adjust the seasoning with more fish sauce or salt if needed. You can also add a beef cube or beef broth for additional flavor if desired.

  5. Once everything is cooked and seasoned to your liking, ladle the Beef Bulalo into serving bowls, making sure to include a generous portion of beef shanks, bone marrow, and vegetables in each bowl.

  6. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy your comforting Beef Bulalo soup!

    7. Note: Beef Bulalo is often enjoyed with a dipping sauce made of fish sauce, calamansi (Filipino lime), and chili peppers. Adjust the dipping sauce according to your taste preference.
